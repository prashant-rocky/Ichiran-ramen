Our Story

A Journey of Flavor and Tradition

Ichiran Ramen began with a simple vision: to serve authentic Japanese ramen that captures the true spirit of Shinjuku. Every bowl we create reflects decades of passion, tradition, and craftsmanship.

We carefully select premium ingredients, slow-cook our broths to perfection, and handcraft noodles that bring comfort in every bite. Our dedication ensures each guest experiences the harmony of flavor and culture.

More than just a meal, Ichiran Ramen is an invitation to enjoy Japan’s culinary heritage. Step inside, and discover a place where warmth, taste, and tradition meet.
